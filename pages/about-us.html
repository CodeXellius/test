About Us

Sabji Mandu Story

Sabji Mandu Supermarket is a family-operated and owned premier grocery chain in Nepal. We carry fresh produce, fruits and vegetables with a wide selection of groceries, specialty groceries, and the largest selections of Indian products anywhere in Nepal. We also carry a wide range of household utensils and appliances for cooking. It is your local one-stop-shop where you can tick off all the grocery items off your grocery list. Our products are presented in an intuitive way which makes it easier for customers to find.

What we offer?

Our entire business revolves around prioritizing customer happiness. We invest heavily in training our employees to provide exceptional service and curate a premium shopping experience. From the moment someone steps into our store, whether it's their first visit or they're a regular customer, they're greeted with warmth and guided through a seamless shopping journey. Our unwavering commitment extends to sourcing only the freshest produce and highest quality products from the best producers and vendors, both locally and internationally. By prioritizing excellence at every touchpoint, we aim to exceed customer expectations and foster long-lasting relationships built on trust and satisfaction.
