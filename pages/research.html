Home Page

While creating the home page for Sabji-Mandu's website, I wanted to build a very minimalist yet captivating webpage. I think that a home page to any website must be simple and should hold the precision of the content held by the entire website.

So, I came across the website for Starbucks Coffee as an inspiration for Sabji-Mandu's home page.

Starting from the navigation menu, Starbucks Coffee's home page had highlighted simple yet user friendly navigation settings for their webpage which inspired me to create a modest and easily accessible navigation menu which included the navigation for the other web pages displayed on Sabji-Mandu's website along with the icons for wish lists, my cart and login or sign up for user friendliness and I had added the logo of our grocery shop in the top left corner of the home page.

The body of Sabji-Mandu's homepage was created using different sections for the vegetables, fruits, instant foods, kitchen essentials and dairy products displayed on the product page of our website. I used vibrant colors and high-quality images that reflected the freshness of our products which is also inspired by Starbuck's website.

In conclusion, I was able to create a user-friendly and appealing home page which reflected the freshness of the products and brand identity of our grocery store by drawing inspiration from Starbucks Coffee's website

Blog Page

For blog, I took references from KISAN webpage. The reason for choosing the KISAN as it is quite simple. The message that was supposed to deliver was delivered quite effectively. There was no extra thing for showoff.

In KISAN, flex is used to display the things .So, I used flex box for displaying my content as well. In 1st flex box, text is displayed at the right side and image is displayed is displayed at left side. Similarly, in 2nd flex box, image is displayed at right side and text is displayed at left side and viceversa in the remaining flex boxes. So, I used this idea for my blog page as well. Similarly, small images was used in this web page and I also took this idea and they used background image so, I used a simple background image as well.
